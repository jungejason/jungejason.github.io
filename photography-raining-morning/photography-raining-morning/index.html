Rainy Morning

It’s a raining morning. It’s still a little dark and I thought it would be an interesting shooting experience.

I used the A7rv camera and the Tokina 50-250mm f4-5.6 lens. It’s training, so I didn’t change lens at all. This lens is sharp as a vintage lens. The macro functionality is also fun to use.
