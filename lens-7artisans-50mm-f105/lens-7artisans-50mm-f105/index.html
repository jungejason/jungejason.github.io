Lens 7Artisans 50mm f1.05

December 29, 2023

tags: lens, a7rv, 7artisans-50mm-f105,

This is a fast lens from China. It’s for full frame camera. My copy is for Sony e mount.

The lens is sharp. Sharp enough wide open. The build is great. I like it very much!!
